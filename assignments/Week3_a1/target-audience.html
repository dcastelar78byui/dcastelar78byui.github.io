Description of the target audience

-Adults,(+18 years) men and women over 18 who visit the mountain peaks and caves of this region.
- Groups of Scouts who visit and camp in this region.

Scenarios

In summer:


- Maximum temperatures to know how much water should be taken in visits to the caves and mountain peaks.
- Possibility of storms during the journey or hike in the mountains.
- Hours of sunrise and sunset, so that we are in a safe place at dusk.
- Cancellation of outdoor activities due to the probability of rain and / or storms.

In winter:


-Minimum temperatures to know how warm they should be.
-Probability of snowstorms.
-Information of the amount of snow that fell or fell to foresee different actions to perform.
-Situation and / or cuts of the routes as well as the avenues due to storms.
-Alert and warning of the arrival of a polar cold storm.
